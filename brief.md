a multiplayer game called "seven Spade"

### 🎯 Objective

End the game with the **lowest total points** from **face-down cards**.

---

### 🧍 Players

* 4 Players
* Use a standard 52-card deck
* No teams

---

### 🎴 Card Values (for points)

| Card | Value                                                  |
| ---- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2–10 | Same as number                                         |
| J    | 11                                                     |
| Q    | 12                                                     |
| K    | 13                                                     |
| A    | 1 or 14 (depends on where it’s used to close sequence) |

---

### 🕹 Game Flow

1. **Starting the Game**

   * Shuffle and deal **13 cards to each player**.
   * The player who holds the **7♠ (Seven of Spades)** must play it face-up to the center of the table to begin.
   * Play proceeds **clockwise** from the player who played 7♠.

2. **Valid Moves on Turn**
   Each turn, a player must do **one of the following**:

   * **Play a card in sequence**:

     * Build the sequence up or down from existing cards in the same suit.
     * E.g., After 7♠, valid cards are 6♠ or 8♠.
   * **Start a new suit** with another **7** (e.g., 7♥, 7♦, 7♣).
   * **Play a face-down card** if unable to play or start any sequence.

3. **Face-Down Cards (Penalty)**

   * If you can’t play or start a new 7, you must place **1 card face-down** in front of you.
   * This card's **value counts as your point penalty** at the end.

4. **Closing a Suit**

   * A suit is **closed** once an **Ace is placed** on either end:

     * If sequence ends at **A after 2**, Ace is worth **1 point**.
     * If ends at **A after K**, Ace is worth **14 points**.
   * Once closed, **no other cards from that suit can be played**.

5. **Closing Rule Consistency**

   * The **first time** an Ace closes a suit (up or down), that **method must be used** for **all suits**.

     * Example: If Spades are closed using A after King (14), all other suits must also close A after King (not after 2).

---

### 🏁 End of Game

* Game ends when all players run out of cards.
* Each player reveals their **face-down penalty cards**.
* Add the **total points** from face-down cards.

### 🥇 Winner

* Player with the **lowest total point value** wins.
* In case of a tie, players with lowest score **share the win**.

---

### ✅ Example Turn

* 1st Player plays 7♠.
* 2nd Player plays 6♠ (goes lower).
* 3rd Player has no Spades or 7s → plays face-down 10♦ → gets 10 points.
* 4th Player plays 8♠.
